## 📈 Executive Summary & Leadership Impact
As the **Consulting Team Lead**, I orchestrated the end-to-end client engagement, framework delivery, and strategic direction for Coffee Company Hada—a design-led specialty coffee boutique positioned between artisan craftsmanship and premium retail. 

While the brand enjoys strong offline customer affinity, its online Shopify storefront faced critical conversion bottlenecks and limited recurring retention. I managed a 4-person cross-functional consulting team (covering communications, business analysis, and market research) to audit their digital ecosystem and build a structured, 10-week D2C growth framework designed to lift total Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) and lower Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC).

---

## 🔬 The 4-Pillar Strategic Framework

Under my leadership, our team developed and proposed a multi-track digital transformation strategy:

### 1. Diagnose & Optimize (Conversion Rate Optimization)
* **Objective:** Audit the end-to-end digital customer journey and remove friction points in the checkout funnel.
* **Execution:** Supervised the redesign guidelines for product landing pages using rich visual storytelling, transparent sourcing timelines, and an optimized, one-click checkout framework to minimize cart abandonment.

### 2. Acquire High-Intent (Performance Marketing & Community)
* **Objective:** Maximize digital marketing ROI and target consumers matching the brand's premium design ethos.
* **Execution:** Developed the target customer personas and mapped hyper-targeted paid social media funnels focused on design and lifestyle audiences to systematically drive down Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC).

### 3. Retain & Repeat (Predictable Subscription Models)
* **Objective:** Stabilize monthly recurring revenue (MRR) and scale individual cohort LTV.
* **Execution:** Formulated structured recurring coffee subscription paths, curated gift bundles, and a premium tiered loyalty program ("Hada Notes") that systematically incentivizes repeat purchasing behaviors.

### 4. Reinforce Culture (Omnichannel Integration)
* **Objective:** Bridge the physical brick-and-mortar retail boutique experience with online storefront visibility.
* **Execution:** Structured localized cultural collaborations with art galleries, independent bookshops, and creative community pop-ups using customized in-café QR code attributions to drive offline-to-online digital onboarding.

## ⏱️ Managed 10-Week Implementation Roadmap
I structured and managed the agile consulting timeline to ensure synchronized delivery across a 4-phase milestone architecture:
* **Weeks 1–2 (Diagnose):** Comprehensive market audit, customer journey logging, and competitive landscape baseline analysis.
* **Weeks 3–5 (Build):** Shopify storefront UX redesign principles, copywriting restructuring, and subscription landing page wireframing.
* **Weeks 6–8 (Prove):** A/B testing frameworks for product pages, beta-group testing of subscription tiers, and deployment of early cultural marketing campaigns.
* **Weeks 9–10 (Scale):** Marketing budget allocation models, performance tracking dashboard finalization, and full handoff to the client.

## 📊 Core Performance Framework (KPIs Tracked)
To establish operational accountability for the client, I designed a performance dashboard measuring critical core business metrics:
* **Conversion Rate:** Total percentage improvement across the Shopify e-commerce checkout funnel.
* **LTV : CAC Ratio:** Balance metric tracking sustainable unit-economic ratios across digital marketing cohorts.
* **Retention Dynamics:** 60-day and 90-day subscription cohort retention percentages and active monthly account net additions.
* **Omnichannel Attribution:** Café-to-online digital conversion rates tracked via unique physical-print QR codes.
